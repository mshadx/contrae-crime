A world where anyone can navigate cybercrime laws with ease

In 2025, a group of Grade 12 students from STI College Fairview created Cybercrime e-Laws to simplify access to cybercrime laws for students and non-experts. Our platform, Weigh the Right, helps users in the Philippines understand their digital rights.

Our Story

January 2025 – A School Project Begins

Founded by Benz Daniel A. Buhay, Zachary Benidict E. Candelario, Jorilyne P. Lanuzo, Jeremy D. Montalban, Sam Othaniel P. Pascual, Elijia R. Ramirez, Matthew M. Valencia, and Hanna Daniella S. Ventura for a Grade 12 ICT project at STI College Fairview.

March 2025 – Building The Website

Developed Weigh the Right for Contra E-Crime, a website offering accessible cybercrime law guidance with a focus on user-friendliness and affordability for students.

May 2025 – Ready for the SHS Expo

Prepared to showcase Weigh the Right at the SHS expo, targeting students and educators with real-time updates on Philippine cyber laws.
